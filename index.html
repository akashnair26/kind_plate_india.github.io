WHAT WE DO

As an effort towards eradicating hunger, we propose a system wherein we collect “surplus” food ( which otherwise goes to the dump even if it’s perfectly edible ), deliver it to NGOs / Care homes etc to ensure that the food reaches the deserving communities. We will basically have an app backed by a logistics network which connects the excess food from caterers to deserving communities having food requirements. If they do not have a requirement, we will directly distribute it to deserving local communities via temporary kiosks in areas where such a service is of utmost need, for a very minimal cost. The locations will be identified with help of NGOs already working in this area.
To address the food wastage problem in restaurants and other such food outlets, we plan to introduce a marketplace model wherein the restaurants can sell their unsold surplus food for a discounted price and customers can directly come and claim this food from the food outlet. To make sure that quality food is served, routine quality audits of the food outlets partnering with us will be held. Through these methods, we aim at bridging the food accessibility gap.
